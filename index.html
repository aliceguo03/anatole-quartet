Who We Are

The Anatole Quartet emerged from the UCSD chamber music scene in Fall 2025, founded by four students who share a dedication to making great music and enjoying the process.

Self-directed and student-run, the quartet regularly performs at the Department of Music Undergraduate Forums and has appeared as guests of the La Jolla Symphony & Chorus.
